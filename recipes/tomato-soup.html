Creamy Tomato Soup

A bowl of creamy tomato soup, with added coconut milk and garnished with parsley

Tomato soup is a very popular dish, and can be modified in many ways. From instant, canned tomato soup to homemade, the dish is popular coupled with grilled cheese sandwiches, as well as salads. Hearty and warm, this delicious soup will fill your home with the aroma of roasted tomatoes coupled with the smell of fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • chicken/vegetable stock
  • tomatoes
  • onion
  • oil
  • garlic
  • salt and pepper
  • basil
  • coconut milk or evaporated milk

Instructions

  1. Lightly drizzle oil onto the tomatoes, onion, and garlic and roast until lightly golden and aromatic
  2. Blend the roasted tomatoes, onion, and garlic in the blender until smooth
  3. Slowly add in the basil, chicken stock, and spices and blend through again
  4. Add soup to pan on the stove and heat on medium, slowly mixing in the coconut milk
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
