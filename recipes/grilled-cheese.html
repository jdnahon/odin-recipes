Grilled Cheese

A warm sandwich made on sourdough with a crunchy crust and a delectable, gooey, and cheesy interior

The go to meal for many, grilled cheese sandwiches are buttery, cheesy bites of heaven. A short and simple recipe, all you need is one or more cheeses, some butter, and bread (preferably sourdough). It goes great with tomato soup, or eaten alone. You can even add extra ingredients in to make it extra delicious!

Ingredients

  • Cheese/cheeses (sliced, shredded, or grated)
  • Two slices of bread (sourdough or other)
  • Room tempearture butter
  • Optional: Any other toppings for the sandwich (meat, avocado, etc)

Steps

  1. Heat up skillet on medium heat over stove with butter
  2. Spread butter on both sides of both pieces of bread
  3. Toast both pieces of bread lightly on one side
  4. Flip pieces of bread and put cheese(s) on top of slightly toasted side
  5. Put lid over skillet so cheese melts evenly
  6. After both sides are melty, use a spatula to stack the bread, putting the cheesy sides together
  7. Optional: Add anything else you would like in the sandwich
  8. Flip occasionally until both peices of bread are a golden brown
  9. Eat the buttery, cheesy deliciousness and enjoy
